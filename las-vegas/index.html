Provider Questions

Las Vegas Provider FAQ

What does it cost to join?

Nothing for founding providers. Founding providers pay zero platform fees and keep 100% of the revenue from every appointment. The platform provides your EHR, scheduling, AI simulator, and patient acquisition at no cost during the founding period.

How do I get patients?

Aesthetics To Go drives patient demand through geo-targeted digital marketing, SEO, and social media across the Las Vegas metro. Patients book through the platform and you receive appointments in your chosen service areas.

Can I work part-time?

Yes. You set your own schedule and availability. Many providers start with evenings and weekends alongside their existing clinical role. The smart scheduling system lets you control exactly when and where you see patients.

What credentials do I need?

Active PA-C, NP, or MD/DO license in good standing in Nevada. Your own malpractice insurance and a collaborative agreement as required by Nevada state law. Credentialing through the platform takes 5-7 business days.

Do I need my own supplies?

You can bring your own or access wholesale pricing through the platform. Aesthetics To Go offers volume pricing on Botox, fillers, and clinical supplies — significantly below retail cost. You purchase what you need, when you need it.
