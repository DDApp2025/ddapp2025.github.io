Market Opportunity

Why Summerlin Is a Premium Market

High household income = high demand.

Summerlin residents have significantly above-average household incomes. These are families and professionals who already invest in aesthetic treatments — and they will pay a premium for the convenience of concierge care. The patient base is affluent, educated, and ready.

Convenience-first community.

Summerlin's master-planned lifestyle is built around convenience — shopping, dining, recreation all walkable. Residents expect premium services to come to them. Mobile aesthetic care fits the Summerlin lifestyle perfectly. You bring the clinic to their home office or kitchen island.

Privacy matters to this demographic.

In a close-knit, village-oriented community, many residents prefer not to be seen walking into an aesthetic clinic. Mobile treatment at home offers complete discretion. This is a significant competitive advantage over traditional clinics — and a key selling point for Summerlin patients.

Dense territory = efficient scheduling.

Summerlin's planned layout means neighborhoods are clustered and easy to navigate. You can see multiple patients per day within a compact geographic area. Less windshield time, more appointment time. The smart scheduling system optimizes your route for maximum efficiency.

Summerlin Provider FAQ

What does it cost to join?

Nothing for founding providers. Founding providers pay zero platform fees and keep 100% of revenue. The platform provides your EHR, scheduling, AI simulator, and patient acquisition tools at no cost during the founding period.

Can I serve just Summerlin?

Yes. You choose your own service territory. You can focus exclusively on Summerlin communities or expand to cover Las Vegas, Henderson, and Boulder City as well. Your territory is entirely up to you.

Can I work part-time?

Yes. You set your own schedule and availability. Many providers start with evenings and weekends alongside their existing clinical role. The platform's smart scheduling lets you control exactly when you see Summerlin patients.

How do Summerlin patients find me?

Aesthetics To Go drives patient demand through geo-targeted digital marketing and SEO specifically targeting Summerlin residents. Patients book through the platform and you receive appointments in your service territory.

Do I need my own supplies?

You can bring your own or access wholesale pricing through the platform. We offer volume pricing on Botox, fillers, and clinical supplies — significantly below retail. Purchase what you need, when you need it.
