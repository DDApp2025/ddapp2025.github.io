Requirements

What We Look For

Non-negotiable clinical standards.
1

Active Professional License

You must hold a current, unrestricted license as a PA-C, NP, or MD/DO in the state where you intend to practice. No exceptions. Every license is verified during our credentialing process before you can accept appointments.

2

Aesthetic Injection Experience

Aesthetics To Go is a clinical operating platform, not a training program. You must have training and hands-on experience in aesthetic injections including neurotoxins and dermal fillers. We recommend AAAMS, IAPAM, or other accredited certification programs for providers entering the field.

3

Malpractice Insurance

All providers must carry their own professional liability and malpractice insurance with coverage adequate for the aesthetic procedures they perform. Proof of active insurance is required during onboarding.

4

Supervisory Compliance

PA-Cs and NPs must maintain a valid collaborative or supervisory agreement as required by their state's practice act. The platform's EHR produces exportable SOAP notes and treatment records for supervising physician chart review.

5

Professional Standards

We expect providers to follow the same clinical protocols used in medical offices ‚Äî sterile technique, FDA-approved products only, proper cold-chain storage, infection control, and emergency preparedness including hyaluronidase for filler complications.

6

Patient-First Mindset

Mobile aesthetic care is built on trust. We look for providers who prioritize clinical outcomes over sales volume, communicate transparently with patients, and treat every home visit with the professionalism of a medical office encounter.

Common Questions

Careers FAQ

What credentials do I need to join?

You must hold an active, unrestricted license as a PA-C, NP, or MD/DO. You also need current malpractice insurance, a valid collaborative or supervisory agreement as required by your state, and training and experience in aesthetic injections.

Am I an employee or independent contractor?

All Aesthetics To Go providers are 1099 independent contractors. You set your own prices, choose your patients, define your service menu, control your schedule and travel radius, and make all clinical decisions. The platform provides the technology ‚Äî you run your practice.

Can I keep my current clinical job?

Absolutely. The platform is designed for providers building a concierge side practice. You control your availability, travel radius, and patient volume. Many providers start with evenings and weekends and scale up as their mobile panel grows.

What if I'm new to aesthetics?

You must be trained and experienced in aesthetic injections before joining. Aesthetics To Go is a clinical operating platform, not a training program. We recommend AAAMS, IAPAM, or other accredited injection certification programs for providers entering the field.

Where are you currently recruiting?

We're actively recruiting founding providers in the Las Vegas metropolitan area ‚Äî Las Vegas, Summerlin, Henderson, Boulder City, North Las Vegas, Spring Valley, Enterprise, and Paradise. The platform is built for expansion to additional markets.

How long does onboarding take?

Our credentialing team reviews applications within 48 hours. Once your license, insurance, and supervisory documentation are verified, you set up your provider profile and treatment menu. Most providers are ready to see their first patients within two weeks of application.
