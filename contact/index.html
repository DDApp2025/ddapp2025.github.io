Common Questions

Provider & General FAQ

How do I apply as a provider?

Visit our providers page to learn about the Provider opportunity and submit your application, or go directly to the provider application. You can also use the form on this page and select "Provider Application" as your inquiry type.

What credentials do I need?

Aesthetics To Go is open to licensed physicians, NPs, and PAs with aesthetic experience. You must hold an active license in your state of practice and carry appropriate malpractice insurance. Experience with injectables (Botox, fillers) is required.

Is there a cost to join?

Providers retain the full payout on completed treatments during the launch period. The transition to a transparent post-launch fee schedule is disclosed in the agreement, not after the fact.

How quickly will I hear back?

We review every inquiry personally and respond within 24 hours. Provider applications are prioritized and typically receive a follow-up call or email the same business day.

Ready to build your mobile practice?

Provider spots are limited. Join a credentialed provider network on a mobile clinical operating system — you set your schedule, your prices, and your service areas. We handle the technology, the credentialed lead flow, and the back-office infrastructure.
