Common Questions

Booking & Contact FAQ

How quickly can I get an appointment?

Most patients receive provider confirmation within 24 hours of submitting a request. Same-day availability depends on provider schedules in your area. The platform shows real-time availability once your request is confirmed.

Is there a travel fee for mobile visits?

No. Aesthetics To Go does not charge travel fees or surcharges for visits within our service areas. Pricing is transparent and set by each provider ‚Äî you see the full cost before booking.

Do I need a consultation before treatment?

A clinical consultation is included as part of every first visit. Your provider assesses your goals, reviews your medical history, and develops a personalized treatment plan. You can also try the free AI simulator beforehand to visualize potential results.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit and debit cards processed through the platform. Insurance is not accepted ‚Äî aesthetic treatments are elective and cash-pay only. Pricing is fully transparent before you book.

Can I cancel or reschedule?

Yes. Cancellation and rescheduling policies are set by each provider and communicated when your appointment is confirmed. Most providers offer flexible rescheduling with reasonable notice.

What if I'm not sure which treatment I need?

Select "Multiple Treatments / Not Sure" in the form and describe your goals. Your provider will help determine the best approach during your consultation. The AI simulator can also help you visualize different treatment options on your own face before your appointment.
