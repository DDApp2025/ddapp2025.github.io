Common Questions

Provider & General FAQ

How do I apply as a provider?

Visit our providers page to learn about founding provider benefits and submit your application, or go directly to the provider application. You can also use the form on this page and select "Provider Application" as your inquiry type.

What credentials do I need?

Aesthetics To Go is open to licensed PA-Cs, NPs, and MDs with aesthetic experience. You must hold an active license in the state of Nevada and carry appropriate malpractice insurance. Experience with injectables (Botox, fillers) is required.

Is there a cost to join?

Zero platform fees for founding providers. No monthly subscription, no per-appointment charges, and no merchant processing fees. You keep your revenue. This is a limited-time founding provider offer for our Las Vegas launch.

How quickly will I hear back?

We review every inquiry personally and respond within 24 hours. Provider applications are prioritized and typically receive a follow-up call or email the same business day.

Ready to build your mobile practice?

Founding provider spots are limited. Join the platform that works like Uber for aesthetic care — you set your schedule, your prices, and your service areas. We handle the technology, the patients, and the back office.
