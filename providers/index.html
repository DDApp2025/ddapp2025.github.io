Common Questions

Provider FAQ

What credentials do I need?

You must hold an active, unrestricted license as a PA-C, NP, or MD/DO. You need your own malpractice insurance and a valid collaborative or supervisory agreement as required by your state. We verify all credentials during onboarding.

Can I do this alongside my clinical job?

Absolutely. The platform is designed for providers building a concierge side practice. You control your schedule, travel radius, and patient volume. Many providers start with evenings and weekends and scale up as their panel grows.

How does the supervising physician relationship work?

You maintain your own collaborative agreement with your supervising physician. The platform's EHR produces clean, exportable SOAP notes and treatment records that can be shared for chart review as required by your state's practice act.

What if I'm new to aesthetics?

You must be trained and experienced in aesthetic injections before joining. Aesthetics To Go is a clinical operating platform, not a training program. We recommend AAAMS, IAPAM, or other accredited injection certification programs for providers entering the field.

How do patients find me?

Patients download the Aesthetics To Go app, run the AI simulator on their face, and book appointments with credentialed providers in their area. The platform handles discovery, scheduling, and payment â€” you focus on clinical care.

Am I an employee or independent contractor?

You are a 1099 independent contractor. You set your own prices, choose your patients, define your service menu, and make all clinical decisions. Aesthetics To Go provides the technology infrastructure â€” you run your practice.
