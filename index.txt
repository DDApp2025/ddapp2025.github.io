Aesthetics To Go — The Clinical Operating System for Aesthetic Providers

Aesthetics To Go (ATG) is a nationwide mobile clinical platform for licensed
aesthetic providers (PA-Cs, NPs, MDs). The platform combines a HIPAA-compliant
mobile EHR, an AI-powered patient simulator, and an injector-priced clinical
supplies catalog (exosomes, post-procedure devices, skincare actives, peptide
supplements, PRP/PRF equipment, transdermal patches, and regenerative biologics).

Founding-provider applications are open. Provider availability varies by market.
