Treatment Details

What to Expect

Before Your Appointment

Simple preparation.

Avoid blood thinners (aspirin, ibuprofen, fish oil) and alcohol for 24 hours before your treatment. No other preparation is needed. Your provider brings everything — you just need a chair and good lighting.

During Treatment

Quick, precise injections.

Most Botox treatments take 10–15 minutes of actual injection time. Your provider uses fine-gauge needles for minimal discomfort. They document injection sites, unit counts, and lot numbers in real-time using the mobile EHR.

After Treatment

Minimal downtime.

Stay upright for 4 hours and avoid rubbing the treated area. You can return to normal activities immediately. Results typically appear within 3–7 days and last 3–4 months.

Follow-Up

Ongoing care on your schedule.

Schedule your next appointment through the app when you're ready. Your complete treatment history, before-and-after photos, and SOAP notes are stored securely in your HIPAA-compliant patient record for continuity of care.

Common Questions

Mobile Botox FAQ

Is at-home Botox safe?

Yes, when administered by a licensed provider. Our PA-Cs and NPs follow identical clinical protocols used in medical offices — sterile technique, proper product handling, HIPAA documentation, lot tracking, and structured SOAP charting. The only difference is the setting.

Who will be treating me?

Every Aesthetics To Go provider is a licensed PA-C, NP, or MD/DO who has been credentialed and verified by our clinical team. All providers carry their own malpractice insurance and maintain supervisory agreements as required by Nevada law. You can view provider credentials before booking.

How much does mobile Botox cost?

Pricing is set by each individual provider and is shown transparently before you book. Because mobile providers have no office overhead, many offer rates competitive with or below traditional clinic pricing. There are no travel fees or convenience surcharges.

How long does Botox last?

Results typically appear within 3–7 days and last 3–4 months. Your provider will recommend a treatment schedule based on your goals and facial anatomy. The app tracks your treatment history for optimal timing on follow-up appointments.

What is the AI simulator?

The AI diagnostic simulator maps realistic Botox outcomes onto your own face using high-fidelity visual modeling. Upload a photo, select your treatment areas, and see a personalized before-and-after visualization — before you ever book an appointment.

Can I book same-day?

Subject to provider availability, same-day appointments may be available. The app shows real-time provider schedules and you can request the earliest available time slot. Most patients are able to book within 24–48 hours.
