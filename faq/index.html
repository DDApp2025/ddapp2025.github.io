For Patients — General

What is Aesthetics To Go?

A mobile clinical operating system that connects you with licensed physicians, NPs, and PAs who deliver Botox, dermal fillers, and hair restoration treatments at your home or office. You book a credentialed provider through Aesthetics To Go, and the provider comes to you with a full clinical kit and HIPAA-compliant documentation.

Where do you operate?

Aesthetics To Go is a nationwide mobile platform. Provider availability varies by market — sign up to be notified when a provider serves your area.

Who will be treating me?

Every provider is a licensed physician (MD/DO), nurse practitioner, or physician assistant who has been credentialed by our team. All carry malpractice insurance and maintain supervisory agreements as required by their state of practice. You can view credentials before booking.

Is at-home treatment safe?

Yes. Our providers follow the same clinical protocols used in medical offices — sterile technique, FDA-approved products, HIPAA documentation, lot tracking, and emergency protocols. The only difference is the setting.

For Patients — Services & Treatments

What services are available?

Botox (forehead, crow's feet, frown lines, brow lift, lip flip, bunny lines, chin, neck bands), dermal fillers (lips, cheeks, jawline, nasolabial folds, marionette lines, under-eye, chin, temples), and hair restoration (PRP therapy).

How much does it cost?

Pricing is set by each provider and shown before you book. No travel fees or surcharges. Because mobile providers have no office overhead, many offer rates competitive with or below clinic pricing.

How long does Botox last?

Results appear within 3–7 days and typically last 3–4 months. The app tracks your treatment history for optimal follow-up timing.

How long do fillers last?

Lip fillers: 6–12 months. Cheek and jawline: 12–18 months. Nasolabial folds: 9–12 months. Results are visible immediately.

Can fillers be reversed?

Yes. Hyaluronic acid fillers can be dissolved with hyaluronidase. All ATG providers carry hyaluronidase as part of their emergency kit.

How does PRP hair restoration work?

A small blood sample is drawn, processed in a portable centrifuge to isolate growth factors, and injected into thinning areas of the scalp. Most plans involve 3–4 sessions over 12 weeks. Results appear in 3–6 months.

For Patients — App & Technology

What does the patient app do?

Browse credentialed providers, view transparent pricing, book appointments, receive reminders, communicate securely with your provider, make payments, view treatment history and before-and-after photos, and schedule follow-ups.

Is my data private?

Yes. All data — photos, medical records, payments — is encrypted and stored in a HIPAA-compliant environment. Only you and your treating provider can see your records. You can delete your data at any time.

Can I book same-day?

Subject to provider availability. The app shows real-time schedules. Most patients book within 24–48 hours.

For Providers

What does it cost to join?

Aesthetics To Go provides a full clinical operating system as part of the Regional Lead Provider agreement. Regional Lead Providers retain the full payout on completed treatments during the launch period, with banking and merchant processing fees absorbed. The transition to a transparent post-launch fee schedule is disclosed in the agreement.

What credentials do I need?

Active, unrestricted license as a physician (MD/DO), nurse practitioner, or physician assistant. Your own malpractice insurance. Valid collaborative or supervisory agreement where state scope requires it. Training and experience in aesthetic injections.

What does the provider app include?

Full mobile EHR with SOAP charting, digital consents, injection site mapping, lot/expiration tracking, before-and-after photos, smart scheduling with route optimization, patient management, integrated payments with banking and merchant fees absorbed during the launch period for Regional Lead Providers, wholesale supply ordering, and exportable records.

Can I do this alongside my clinical job?

Absolutely. You control your schedule, travel radius, and patient volume. Many Regional Lead Providers start with evenings and weekends, scaling from a single patient per month to a full caseload as their panel grows.

Am I an employee or independent contractor?

Independent contractor (1099). You set your prices, choose your patients, define your services, and make all clinical decisions. ATG provides the technology — you run your practice.

How do patients find me?

Patients download the app and book with credentialed providers in their area. The operating system handles discovery, scheduling, and payments. You focus on clinical care.
