For Patients — General

What is Aesthetics To Go?

A mobile clinical platform that connects you with licensed PA-Cs, NPs, and MDs who deliver Botox, dermal fillers, and hair restoration treatments at your home or office. Think of it as Uber for aesthetic medicine — you book, a credentialed provider comes to you.

Where do you operate?

Las Vegas, Summerlin, Henderson, Boulder City, North Las Vegas, Spring Valley, Enterprise, and Paradise. Additional service areas are opening soon.

Who will be treating me?

Every provider is a licensed PA-C, NP, or MD/DO who has been credentialed by our team. All carry malpractice insurance and maintain supervisory agreements as required by Nevada law. You can view credentials before booking.

Is at-home treatment safe?

Yes. Our providers follow the same clinical protocols used in medical offices — sterile technique, FDA-approved products, HIPAA documentation, lot tracking, and emergency protocols. The only difference is the setting.

For Patients — Services & Treatments

What services are available?

Botox (forehead, crow's feet, frown lines, brow lift, lip flip, bunny lines, chin, neck bands), dermal fillers (lips, cheeks, jawline, nasolabial folds, marionette lines, under-eye, chin, temples), and hair restoration (PRP therapy).

How much does it cost?

Pricing is set by each provider and shown before you book. No travel fees or surcharges. Because mobile providers have no office overhead, many offer rates competitive with or below clinic pricing.

How long does Botox last?

Results appear within 3–7 days and typically last 3–4 months. The app tracks your treatment history for optimal follow-up timing.

How long do fillers last?

Lip fillers: 6–12 months. Cheek and jawline: 12–18 months. Nasolabial folds: 9–12 months. Results are visible immediately.

Can fillers be reversed?

Yes. Hyaluronic acid fillers can be dissolved with hyaluronidase. All ATG providers carry hyaluronidase as part of their emergency kit.

How does PRP hair restoration work?

A small blood sample is drawn, processed in a portable centrifuge to isolate growth factors, and injected into thinning areas of the scalp. Most plans involve 3–4 sessions over 12 weeks. Results appear in 3–6 months.

For Patients — App & Technology

What does the patient app do?

Run the AI simulator on your face, browse credentialed providers, view transparent pricing, book appointments, receive reminders, communicate securely with your provider, make payments, view treatment history and before-and-after photos, and schedule follow-ups.

What is the AI simulator?

Upload a photo, select treatment areas, and see realistic before-and-after outcomes mapped onto your own face. Free to use, HIPAA-compliant, no obligation. Helps you set clear expectations before booking.

Is my data private?

Yes. All data — photos, medical records, payments — is encrypted and stored in a HIPAA-compliant environment. Only you and your treating provider can see your records. You can delete your data at any time.

Can I book same-day?

Subject to provider availability. The app shows real-time schedules. Most patients book within 24–48 hours.

For Providers

What does it cost to join?

Zero. No monthly fees, no setup fees, no merchant processing fees for founding providers. You keep 100% of your treatment revenue via direct deposit.

What credentials do I need?

Active, unrestricted license as PA-C, NP, or MD/DO. Your own malpractice insurance. Valid collaborative or supervisory agreement. Training and experience in aesthetic injections.

What does the provider app include?

Full mobile EHR with SOAP charting, digital consents, injection site mapping, lot/expiration tracking, before-and-after photos, smart scheduling with route optimization, patient management, zero-fee payments, wholesale supply ordering, and exportable records.

Can I do this alongside my clinical job?

Absolutely. You control your schedule, travel radius, and patient volume. Many providers start with evenings and weekends. Zero monthly overhead means no pressure to fill a schedule.

Am I an employee or independent contractor?

Independent contractor (1099). You set your prices, choose your patients, define your services, and make all clinical decisions. ATG provides the technology — you run your practice.

How do patients find me?

Patients download the app, run the AI simulator, and book with credentialed providers in their area. The platform handles discovery, scheduling, and payments. You focus on clinical care.
