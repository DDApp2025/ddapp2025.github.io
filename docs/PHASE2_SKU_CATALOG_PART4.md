Each bundle is composed of SKUs already detailed in Parts 1–3 (or the existing Phase 1 catalog). Bundle prices are illustrative ranges based on the per-SKU wholesale ranges reported in the underlying parts; final pricing is set by manufacturer account quotes.

### Bundle 1 — Recovery Practice Starter
- **Included SKUs:** Integrative Peptides BPC-157 PURE Immediate Release (`integrative-peptides-bpc157-pure`); Designs for Health Whole Body Collagen (`designs-for-health-whole-body-collagen`); Dp Dermaceuticals EXO-SKIN (existing Phase 1 SKU); AnteAGE MDX Exosome Solution (existing Phase 1 SKU); 10-pack GHK-Cu cosmetic patch (`ghk-cu-copper-peptide-patch`).
- **Clinical rationale:** Layered post-procedure protocol — in-office topical EV finish (AnteAGE MDX), overnight cosmetic patch (GHK-Cu), and patient take-home stack (BPC-157 + collagen + EXO-SKIN). Covers the 0–14 day post-RF-microneedling / post-laser recovery window without requiring a prescriber visit at any step.
- **Provider avatar:** Solo NP or PA running RF microneedling, fractional laser, and chemical peel volume; wants one stack to recommend after every procedure.
- **À la carte wholesale:** ~$430–$580.
- **Bundle target price:** $375–$500 (approx. 12–15% bundle margin captured for ATG plus pass-through discount to provider).
- **Slug:** `bundle-recovery-practice-starter`

## Deliverable 3 — Strategic Flags

- **ZEO MOU exclusivity runs Regen Therapy → ZEO, not ZEO → Regen Therapy.** ZEO's Masterclass and direct-physician sales pipeline continue to operate in parallel. Verify in writing with ZEO BD before assuming a parallel aesthetic-specialty distribution path is open, but the public language is favorable to ATG.
- **July 23, 2026 PCAC ruling** on BPC-157, KPV, MOTS-C, TB-500 (and 24-July: DSIP, Epitalon, Semax) will reshape the peptide market within 90 days of the decision. Hold deep inventory commits on Integrative Peptides BPC and KPV SKUs until the ruling is public; the supplement-pathway argument depends on the acetylated/dipeptide-fragment distinction holding up.
- **February 2027 PCAC ruling** on GHK-Cu, LL-37, Dihexa, PEG-MGF, Melanotan II is the second inflection point; flag in the internal tracker for both the Quicksilver Copper GHK SKU and the GHK-Cu cosmetic patch.
- **Hims & Hers late-2026 longevity launch is the largest competitive signal**, but it threatens the consumer/longevity end of the catalog — not ATG's licensed-injector core. Differentiation lives in equipment + procedural consumables + skincare actives, which Hims will not stock.
- **Florida SB 1768 + Utah SB 199 are the only operative state safe-harbors** for allogeneic perinatal biologics. Texas and Arizona expansion is plausible 2026–2027 but no bills are filed; plan for two-state restriction through end of 2027.
- **Transdermal patch category is ~70% noise.** GHK-Cu cosmetic patch is the only fully defensible play; the Peptual iontophoretic patch is conditionally acceptable with messaging caveats. Carry no more than two patch SKUs at launch.
- **Harvest Technologies → Terumo → Globus Medical.** SmartPrep buyers should source through Globus, not legacy Terumo channels. Surface this if any provider asks for Harvest equipment.
- **Cytora Therapeutics × ZEO partnership (announced 2026-01-07)** signals ZEO is broadening from autologous services into manufacturing allogeneic stem-cell therapy. Expect a different regulatory profile on those future SKUs and possible competing supply pressures on DayZero capacity.
- **Integrative Peptides offers ~40% practitioner discount** vs. the 25–35% peer norm. This is the most catalog-friendly margin structure in the supplement segment and worth flagging when modeling Phase 2 unit economics.
- **ZEO Masterclass is practically required** even for SKUs that do not legally require it. ~70 physicians per monthly cohort, >500 total trained as of April 2026; account activation moves much faster after attendance.
- **DayZero Stem Cells is the highest-risk SKU in the entire Phase 2 catalog.** SB 1768's authorized scope is orthopedic / wound care / pain management — aesthetic indication is off-label and outside the state safe-harbor. If ATG cannot gate provider-by-provider against off-label use, omit at launch and offer DayZero EV only.
- **ZEO GROW X is topical and nationally available** — classify it under Exosomes (topical) rather than Category D Regenerative Biologics for cleaner provider UX. Avoid making it look geo-gated when it is not.
- **Amniotic membrane (361 HCT/P) is the most regulatorily stable Category D SKU.** Open BioTissue + Tides as parallel suppliers from the start; the multi-vendor redundancy is cheap insurance against single-supplier capacity issues.
- **Selphyl, Arkeo, Tailor Made OTC, Corganix, Trevena Health** — all unverifiable in 2026 distribution or duplicative of stronger SKUs already in the priority list. Document the exclusion so they do not reappear in future research cycles.
