Our Clinical Standards

These apply to every provider on the platform.
1

Licensed Providers Only

Every provider on Aesthetics To Go holds an active, unrestricted license as a PA-C, NP, or MD/DO. No exceptions. No estheticians performing injections. No unlicensed personnel. Every license is verified during our credentialing process.

2

Malpractice Insurance Required

All providers must carry their own professional liability and malpractice insurance. Coverage must be active and adequate for the aesthetic procedures they perform. Proof of insurance is verified before any provider can accept appointments.

3

Supervisory Compliance

Providers maintain collaborative or supervisory agreements as required by their state's practice act. The platform's EHR produces clean, exportable SOAP notes and treatment records that can be shared with supervising physicians for chart review.
