Common Questions

AI Simulator FAQ

How accurate is the AI simulator?

The simulator uses high-fidelity visual mapping calibrated against real clinical outcomes. Results are designed to be realistic and conservative. Your provider will review your simulation and may adjust the treatment plan based on your unique facial anatomy. The simulator is a visualization tool, not a clinical guarantee.

Is my photo kept private?

Yes. All photos are encrypted and stored in a HIPAA-compliant environment. Your images are never shared publicly, used for marketing, or visible to anyone other than you and your treating provider. You can delete your data at any time.

Is the simulator free?

Yes. The AI diagnostic simulator is completely free for all patients with no obligation to book a treatment. You can run as many simulations as you want ‚Äî try different treatment areas, compare combinations, and take your time deciding.

What treatments can I simulate?

Currently supported: Botox for forehead lines, crow's feet, frown lines, brow lift, and lip flip. Dermal fillers for lip augmentation, cheek volume, jawline contouring, and nasolabial fold softening. Additional treatment areas are being added regularly.

Do I need to download an app?

The AI simulator is available through the Aesthetics To Go patient app. Download the app, create a free account, and you can start running simulations immediately. No appointment or payment information required.

Can my provider see my simulation?

Only if you book an appointment. When you schedule a treatment, your simulation results are shared with your assigned provider so they can review your goals and prepare the appropriate products before arriving at your home. If you don't book, your simulations remain completely private.
